Our Story

Made slow, on purpose

Antico means old — and that's the point. No industrial mix, no shortcuts, no flavor built in a lab. Every batch starts with real fruit, real nuts, real cream, and enough patience to let the flavor lead.

It's an Italian technique brought to a Moroccan table: the same slow-churned method gelato makers have used for generations, built here around ingredients from this city — saffron threads, Marrakchi almonds, mountain honey.

"The texture and variety are rare to find — and worth every dirham."
